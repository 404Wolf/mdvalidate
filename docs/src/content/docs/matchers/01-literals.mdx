title: Literal

order: 1
---

import SchemaAndInput from "../../../components/SchemaAndInput.astro";

The most basic kind of match you can do in a schema is just plain text. Literal text always validates itself.

<SchemaAndInput schema={`Hello World`} input={`Hello World`} valid={true} />
<SchemaAndInput schema={`# Hi`} input={`# Hi`} valid={true} />

<SchemaAndInput schema={`Hello World`} input={`Hello World`} valid={false} />
<SchemaAndInput schema={`# Hi`} input={`## Hi`} valid={false} />
